Who we are

Our team at Keithson Auto Detailing is passionate about restoring and enhancing vehicles. Our skilled team delivers meticulous attention to detail and exceptional results, providing the highest standard of car care and unparalleled customer service.

Our Detailing Services

Interior Cleaning

Interior detailing is a comprehensive cleaning and restoration process that focuses on the interior of your car. Our skilled technicians clean and sanitize all surfaces, including the dashboard, seats, carpets, and door panels. We also specialize in restoring faded upholstery, removing tough stains, repairing burns and cracks, and eliminating unpleasant odors, ensuring your car's interior looks and feels like new.

Exterior Cleaning

Enhance your car's exterior with our comprehensive exterior detailing service. Our experts perform a thorough cleaning and restoration to bring out the best in your vehicle's appearance. We clean the wheels, tires, and windows, remove contaminants, restore clarity to headlights and taillights, and even treat the trim and plastic surfaces to ensure a complete rejuvenation of your car's exterior.

Specialty Services

We offer a range of specialty services to meet your unique needs. Our experts excel in paint correction, effectively removing scratches, swirl marks, and other imperfections to restore a flawless finish. We also provide headlight restoration to improve visibility and enhance the appearance of your vehicle. Additionally, we offer services such as paint protection coatings, fabric and leather protection, and engine bay cleaning for a comprehensive and tailored car care experience.
