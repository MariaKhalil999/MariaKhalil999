Add-On Services

(Can be selected in the booking process)

Pet Hair Removal

Pet hair removal is a specialized service that focuses on effectively eliminating pet hair from your car's interior. Our skilled technicians utilize various techniques such as specialized brushes, vacuuming, and hand-picking to ensure every trace of pet hair is thoroughly removed, leaving your car clean and free from allergens.

45 mins 30

Paint Correction (2-Step)

Our 2-step paint correction process is a technique aimed at restoring and refining the paintwork of your vehicle. We utilize professional-grade compounds and polishes to remove imperfections such as swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation. This service achieves a smooth, glossy finish that brings back the original shine of your car's paint.

6h 450

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating is a premium protective treatment that forms a durable, transparent layer over your vehicle's paintwork. We offer a comprehensive ceramic coating service that includes thorough paint correction to address imperfections before applying the coating. Our technicians prepare the surface, perform necessary paint correction, and then expertly apply the ceramic coating, providing long-lasting shine, protection against environmental factors, and an enhanced appearance for your vehicle.

7.5h 600

Headlight Restoration

Opting for our headlight restoration service will improve the clarity and functionality of your vehicle's headlights. Our team uses a combination of professional-grade products and techniques to remove oxidation, yellowing, and haze from the headlight lenses. This enhances the appearance of the headlights, improves visibility, and ensures they perform optimally for a safer driving experience.

60 mins 70

Dent Repair

Dent repair restores the shape and appearance of your vehicle's body panels. We use various techniques, such as paintless dent repair (PDR) to effectively remove dents without the need for extensive bodywork or repainting. We use specialized tools to gently reshape the metal, resulting in a seamless repair that restores the original contours of your vehicle.

60 mins 65

Spray Wax Shine

Spray wax shine is a quick and convenient way to enhance the appearance and provide a protective layer to your vehicle's paintwork. We apply a high-quality spray wax product to the exterior surfaces of your car, which is then gently buffed to achieve a glossy shine. This adds a layer of protection against contaminants and UV rays, and leaves your car with a showroom-worthy finish.

30 mins 25
